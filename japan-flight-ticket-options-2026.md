## Recommendation

For the best balance of price, direct flights, no red-eye timing, and 3 checked bags, check in this order:

1. Nagoya (NGO) on China Airlines for all four date ranges.
2. Fukuoka (FUK) on China Airlines or EVA, especially if the final fare is close to Nagoya.
3. Osaka (KIX) with low-cost airlines plus full-service comparison after adding all 3 checked bags.
4. Tokyo (NRT) if you want the biggest city option; avoid TPE-HND low-cost flights that depart around midnight.
5. Hokkaido/Sapporo (CTS) only if the destination is worth the higher budget; China Airlines is the first airline to check.

Avoid booking low-cost flights based on the first fare shown. For this group, compare only the final checkout total after adding 3 checked bags, seat selection if needed, and payment fees.
